Decodr is a Chrome extension that adds folders, tags, imports, a prompt library, and progress tracking to NotebookLM. Think of it as a power toolkit that fills in the gaps NotebookLM hasn't gotten around to yet. Everything lives right inside the NotebookLM page, so it doesn't feel like a separate tool.

There's a free tier that gives you a solid set of features with some limits (2 folders, 3 tags, 5 prompts, etc.). If you need more, Decodr Plus removes all limits. It starts at $2.99/mo and there's a lifetime option for $49 if you'd rather pay once and be done with it. Every paid plan comes with a 7-day free trial, no credit card required.
