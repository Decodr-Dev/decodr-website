### Headline

---

**Q: What is Decodr?**
A: Decodr is a Chrome extension that adds folders, tags, imports, a prompt library, and progress tracking to NotebookLM. Think of it as a power toolkit that fills in the gaps NotebookLM hasn't gotten around to yet. Everything lives right inside the NotebookLM page, so it doesn't feel like a separate tool.

---

**Q: Is Decodr free?**
A: There's a free tier that gives you a solid set of features with some limits (2 folders, 3 tags, 5 prompts, etc.). If you need more, Decodr Plus removes all limits. It starts at $2.99/mo and there's a lifetime option for $49 if you'd rather pay once and be done with it. Every paid plan comes with a 7-day free trial, no credit card required.

---

**Q: How does the free trial work?**
A: You get full access to all Plus features for 7 days. No credit card upfront. Use it like you normally would, try everything, and see if it makes a difference in your workflow. If it does, pick a plan. If not, no hard feelings. You'll just go back to the free tier.

---

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yep. No cancellation fees, no hoops to jump through, no passive aggressive emails. Go to your account settings, click cancel, done. You'll keep Plus until your current billing period ends.

---

**Q: What happens to my stuff if I cancel or downgrade?**
A: Nothing gets deleted. Your notebooks, sources, and everything inside them stays safe. But any folders, tags, or prompts you created beyond the free limits will be hidden until you resubscribe. Your extra stuff just takes a little nap. It's all still there if you come back.

---

**Q: Is the lifetime deal actually lifetime?**
A: Yes. One payment, permanent access to Plus. No renewals, no surprise charges, no fine print that says "lifetime means 3 years." It's the real deal.

---

**Q: Does Decodr access or store my notebook content?**
A: Nope. Decodr runs entirely in your browser. We don't read your notebooks, we don't store your content, and we don't send it anywhere. The only data we handle is your Google sign-in for authentication. That's it.

---

**Q: What browsers does it work on?**
A: Decodr is a Chrome extension, so it works on Chrome and any Chromium-based browser like Edge, Brave, Arc, and Opera.

---

**Q: Will Decodr break if NotebookLM updates?**
A: Possible, but unlikely. We keep an eye on NotebookLM changes and update Decodr to stay compatible. If something breaks after a NotebookLM update, we'll fix it as fast as we can. You can reach us at support@decodr.app if you run into anything weird.

---

**Q: Can I import from ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini?**
A: Yes. Open any conversation in ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, and Decodr lets you import it directly into a NotebookLM notebook with one click. No copy-pasting, no formatting headaches.

---

**Q: Can I import YouTube playlists or entire channels?**
A: On the free tier you can import individual YouTube videos (up to 3/week). With Plus, you get unlimited imports plus the ability to pull in full playlists and entire channels in one go.

---

**Q: Can I import Anki flashcards?**
A: Yes. Decodr handles Anki decks and other file types that NotebookLM doesn't support natively. Just select your file and it takes care of the conversion.

---

**Q: My PDF is too large for NotebookLM. Can Decodr help?**
A: That's literally one of the reasons we built this. Decodr can compress your PDF to fit under NotebookLM's 200MB limit, or split it into smaller parts if compression alone isn't enough. Both options are available on all plans, including Free.

---

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Credit cards and bank transfers, processed securely through Paystack.

---

**Q: I have a feature request.**
A: We love those. Submit it here: [feature request link]. We read every single one and it genuinely shapes what we build next.

---

**Q: Something isn't working. Where do I get help?**
A: Email us at support@decodr.app. We're a small team and we reply fast.

## PAGE 7: PRIVACY POLICY

---

### Headline
Privacy Policy

### Last updated: [Date]

---

### The short version

Decodr runs in your browser. We don't read your notebooks, store your content, or send your data to third parties. The only information we collect is what's needed to make the extension work and process payments.

---

### The longer version

**What we collect**

When you sign in with Google OAuth, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture. This is used for authentication and account management. That's it.

If you purchase Decodr Plus, your payment is processed by Paystack. We do not store your credit card details. Paystack handles all payment data according to their own privacy policy and security standards.

**What we don't collect**

We do not collect, store, read, or transmit:
- The contents of your NotebookLM notebooks
- Your sources, notes, or generated content
- Your browsing history
- Any data from the websites you visit

Decodr operates entirely within your browser. Your notebook data stays between you and NotebookLM (Google).

**How the extension works**

Decodr injects features into the NotebookLM interface. All organization features (folders, tags, prompt library, progress tracking) are stored locally in your browser. Import features work by passing content directly to NotebookLM through the browser. At no point does your content pass through our servers.

**Analytics**

We may collect basic, anonymized usage data such as which features are used most frequently. This helps us understand what to improve. This data is aggregated and never linked to individual users or their content.

**Data deletion**

If you want your account data removed, email support@decodr.app and we'll take care of it.

**Changes to this policy**
